About BLACK OPS II

Call of Duty: Black Ops II takes place between during the

end of the Cold War and during a fictional second cold

war. It was also the first game in the series to feature

futuristic settings, weaponry and technology.


Also for the first time in Call of Duty games, Black Ops II

features branching stories with non-linear gameplay, as well

as multiple endings.




Black Ops II introduces players to a villain named Raul Menendez

who takes control of American military infrastructure to carry

out his plans. Use the power of near-future technology and

advanced robotics to prevent the destruction of the world.
